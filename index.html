Fitness Buddy

This App helps in maintaining a healthy weight.

Obesity can cause many health problems. For maintaining health, productivity and prevention of diet -related diseases. It is important to follow a healthy and balanced diet, preferably a traditional diet and maintain a healthy weight.

This app gives a complete walkthrough to achieve a healthy weight in a nutshell. The BMI, BMR, TDEE and Daily calorie intake are important parameters which can help decide a weight management plan as per convenience, make a note of your exercise plan and Meal Plan.This can help anyone who cannot comply on rigid plans given to them to achieve a healthy weight.

The details of what are limitations of the App has been provided in the disclaimer.This App does not give any medical or general advise but only gives calculations of important parameters and Approximate calorific values.You can download the App from the IOS AppStore.
